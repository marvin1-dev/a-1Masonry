Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it typically take to complete a project?

The timeline will vary depening on the size and complexity of the project. On average, a job can take anywhere from a few days to several weeks to complete.

Why hire a professional masonry service?

A-1 Masonry is skilled in masonry techniques and equipped with specialized tools, ensuring efficient and timely project completion. We prioritize superior workmanship, leading to long-lasting and durable results for our clients.

Do you offer free estimates or consultations?

Yes — we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all projects. We’ll schedule a time to visit your site, discuss your vision, and provide a detailed quote before any work begins.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Catawba, NC and proudly serve the surrounding communities — including Hickory, Statesville, Newton, and the greater Catawba Valley area.

Are your projects covered by warranty?

Yes — we stand by the quality of our craftsmanship. Most of our masonry work includes a workmanship warranty. We’ll be happy to discuss warranty terms with you during your consultation.
