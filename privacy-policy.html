Introduction

A-1 Masonry LLC respects your privacy. This policy outlines what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it.

What We Collect

  • Site usage data: pages visited, browser/device type (via Google Analytics).
How We Use Data

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand website traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties unless legally required or to operate our website securely.

Security

We implement standard security protocols to protect your data, but no online method is completely risk-free.

Your Rights

You can request to review, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us directly.
